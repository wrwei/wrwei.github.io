Welcome to my blog! I have been meaning to set up a space to share thoughts beyond formal publications for a while, and here it is.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about topics close to my research and teaching, including:

- Safety-critical systems engineering and assurance
- Model-based systems engineering (MBSE) in practice
- Digital twins and their applications
- Reflections on academic life and career

## Why Blog?

Academic papers are great for rigorous contributions, but they don't always capture the informal insights, failed experiments, and practical lessons that are equally valuable. This blog is a place for those stories.

> The best way to learn is to teach; the best way to think is to write.

Stay tuned for more posts. Feel free to reach out via email if you'd like to discuss anything!
